Generate your next Dominion set
random, customized and offline.

Get started

Customizable to your needs

Regardless whether you just began to play Dominion or you are an experienced veteran who purchases every expansion instantly on release, choose your expansion(s) of choice and let a suitable kingdom be generated. Unsatisfied with the result? Just reroll the whole set or only this one garsty card your friend is always terrorizing you with. Do all that in your prefered language to easily find the generated cards in your box.

Works everywhere

Even though Dominionizer is accessible with any browser, you don't necessarily need an internet connection to use it. Just install it on your device - phone, tablet or desktop - and be prepared for your next session deep down in your friend's gaming dungeon.

Always up-to-date

Dominionizer is fueled by the comprehensive data set of the Dominion Strategy Wiki which is maintained by a dedicated community. Changes to the wiki are transfered to this app almost weekly. Found a missing translation or an error in a card description? Jump in and help the wiki community out.
